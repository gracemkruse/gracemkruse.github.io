About Me

Hi, my name is Grace Kruse. I love the color blue, whether it’s shibori or cheese. I come from a family of nine children so it feels natural to work in the noisiest of coffee shops. With those important facts out of the way, I'll tell you a bit about myself. I am a senior at Pepperdine University and this school has brought me to the best of all possible worlds, inviting me to study in London and let me analyze some of the worlds finest books. As a trained ballet dancer, I’ve always loved bringing beauty to life but it doesn’t stop with dance. I have combined my passions and brought them to design. In the fall of 2017, I began working as a graphic designer. Whether creating a specimen for the typeface, Mrs. Eaves, or leading a team to code a Harry Potteresque moving portrait, I have found that I belong in the world of design.
