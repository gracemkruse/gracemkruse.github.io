About Me

Hi, my name is Grace Kruse. I love the color blue, whether it’s cyanotype or cheese. I come from a family of nine kids so before COVID-19, I did my best work in noisy coffee shops with plenty of distractions. With those important facts out of the way, I'll tell you a bit about myself. I graduated from Pepperdine University in 2019 which brought me to the best of all possible worlds. It allowed me to study in London, analyze some of the worlds finest books and discover my love for design. In the fall of 2017, I began working as a designer along with emerging technology. Throughout the years I have found myself the most inspired and doing my best work at the convergence of my passions like, design, art, science, and technology. Whether creating a type specimen for Mrs. Eaves, or leading a team to code a Harry Potteresque moving portrait, I am a creator through and through.
